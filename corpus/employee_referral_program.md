
# Sandbox Employee Referral Program

## 1. Introduction

Great people know great people. Our Employee Referral Program is designed to encourage employees to refer talented candidates from their network for open positions at Sandbox.

## 2. Eligibility

- All full-time and part-time employees of Sandbox are eligible to participate in the referral program, except for:
    - Executive leadership team members.
    - HR employees.
    - Hiring managers for their own open positions.

## 3. How it Works

1.  **Check for Openings:** Review our current job openings on the company careers page.
2.  **Submit a Referral:** If you know someone who would be a great fit, submit their resume and a brief description of why you are recommending them through our internal referral portal.
3.  **Candidate Application:** The referred candidate must apply for the position and indicate that they were referred by you.
4.  **Hiring Process:** The candidate will go through our standard hiring process.
5.  **Get Rewarded:** If your referred candidate is hired and successfully completes their 3-month probation period, you will receive a referral bonus.

## 4. Referral Bonus

- **Standard Bonus:** For most positions, the referral bonus is R20,000.
- **Hard-to-Fill Roles:** For certain critical or hard-to-fill roles, an increased bonus may be offered. These roles will be highlighted on the careers page.
- **Payment:** The referral bonus will be paid out, less applicable taxes, in the payroll following the successful completion of the new hire's probation period.

## 5. Program Guidelines

- **Fair Consideration:** All referred candidates will be evaluated against the same criteria as all other applicants.
- **No Limit:** There is no limit to the number of referrals an employee can make.
- **Duplicate Referrals:** If a candidate is referred by more than one employee, the first referral received will be the one eligible for the bonus.
- **Candidate Ownership:** A referral is considered "active" for 6 months. If the candidate is hired for any position within that time, the referring employee is eligible for the bonus.

## 6. Let's Build a Great Team

We believe that our employees are our best asset in finding new talent. We appreciate your help in building a world-class team at Sandbox.
