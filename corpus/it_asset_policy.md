
# Sandbox IT Asset Policy

## 1. Introduction

This policy governs the use and management of all IT assets owned by Sandbox. These assets, including laptops, mobile phones, software, and peripherals, are provided to employees to perform their job duties.

## 2. Asset Allocation and Return

- **Allocation:** IT assets are allocated to employees based on their role and job requirements. All allocated assets are recorded in our asset management system.
- **Return:** Upon termination of employment, all company-owned IT assets must be returned to the IT department in good working condition.

## 3. Acceptable Use

- **Business Use:** IT assets are intended for business use. Limited and occasional personal use is permitted, provided it does not interfere with work, consume significant resources, or violate any other company policies.
- **Prohibited Use:** Company assets must not be used for any illegal activities, to access or distribute inappropriate content, or for any commercial purposes outside of Sandbox business.
- **Software:** Only software that is approved and licensed by Sandbox may be installed on company laptops. The installation of unauthorized software is strictly prohibited.

## 4. Care of Assets

- **Physical Security:** Employees are responsible for the physical security of the assets allocated to them. Assets should not be left unattended in public places. Laptops should be password-protected.
- **Damage or Loss:** Any damage to or loss of an IT asset must be reported to the IT department immediately. A police report may be required in the case of theft.
- **Repairs and Maintenance:** All repairs and maintenance of IT assets must be performed by the IT department or an authorized service provider.

## 5. Mobile Devices

- **Company-Provided Devices:** If you are provided with a company mobile phone, the same rules of acceptable use and care apply.
- **Bring Your Own Device (BYOD):** If you use your personal mobile device to access company email or data, you must adhere to our BYOD policy. This includes having a secure passcode on your device and allowing the company to remotely wipe company data if the device is lost or stolen.

## 6. Policy Compliance

- We reserve the right to monitor the use of our IT assets to ensure compliance with this and other policies.
- Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
