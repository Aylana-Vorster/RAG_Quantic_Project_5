
# Sandbox Remote Work Policy

## 1. Introduction

Sandbox embraces remote work to provide flexibility and support a healthy work-life balance. This policy outlines the guidelines and expectations for employees who work remotely, either fully or on a hybrid schedule.

## 2. Eligibility

Most roles at Sandbox are eligible for remote work. Eligibility for fully remote or hybrid arrangements is determined by job function and is subject to approval by your manager and Human Resources.

## 3. Work Environment and Equipment

- **Workspace:** Remote employees are expected to maintain a safe, ergonomic, and distraction-free work environment.
- **Equipment:** Sandbox will provide the necessary equipment for you to perform your job, including a laptop and other peripherals. Company-provided equipment is for business use and must be protected from damage and unauthorized use.
- **Internet:** A stable, high-speed internet connection is required for remote work. Employees are responsible for their own internet service and costs. A stipend may be provided to offset these costs, as outlined in the Expense Policy.

## 4. Work Hours and Availability

- **Work Hours:** Remote employees are expected to be available and online during their agreed-upon work hours. Core collaboration hours will be established by each team.
- **Communication:** Regular communication with your team and manager is essential. Remote employees are expected to be responsive on company communication channels (e.g., Slack, email) during work hours.

## 5. Security

- **Data Security:** All remote employees must adhere to Sandbox's Information Security Policy to protect company and client data. This includes using a secure Wi-Fi network and a VPN when required.
- **Confidentiality:** Confidential information must be protected at all times. Be mindful of your surroundings and ensure that sensitive conversations and documents are not exposed to unauthorized individuals.

## 6. Hybrid Work

Employees on a hybrid schedule will coordinate with their manager to determine their in-office and remote workdays. The company's goal is to balance the benefits of in-person collaboration with the flexibility of remote work.

## 7. Policy Acknowledgment

All employees working remotely must read and acknowledge this policy. Any questions should be directed to your manager or Human Resources.
