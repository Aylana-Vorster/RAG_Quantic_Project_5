
# Sandbox Code of Conduct

## 1. Introduction

As our CEO, Stacy Fakename, often says, "Our success is built on a foundation of respect and integrity." Sandbox is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all employees, clients, and partners. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior to ensure a safe, respectful, and professional workplace. It applies to all Sandbox employees, contractors, and representatives, whether in a Sandbox office, working remotely, or at company-related events.

## 2. Core Principles

- **Be Respectful:** Treat everyone with courtesy, respect, and professionalism. Disagreements and differing opinions are normal, but they must be handled with civility.
- **Be Inclusive:** We value diversity and are committed to creating an environment where everyone feels welcome and valued, regardless of their background, identity, or beliefs.
- **Be Collaborative:** We achieve more when we work together. Support your colleagues and contribute to a positive team environment.
- **Be Professional:** Uphold Sandbox's reputation through your conduct. This includes all forms of communication, both internal and external.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

- **Harassment and Discrimination:** Any form of harassment or discrimination, including but not limited to that based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, or gender identity.
- **Bullying:** Intimidation, humiliation, or undermining of individuals or groups.
- **Violence and Threats:** Any act or threat of violence, whether verbal or physical.
- **Dishonesty:** Theft, fraud, or any other form of dishonest behavior.
- **Confidentiality Breaches:** Unauthorized disclosure of confidential company or client information.

## 4. Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct, we encourage you to report it. You can report violations to:

- Your direct manager
- Human Resources
- Anonymously through our [internal reporting tool](#)

All reports will be taken seriously and investigated promptly and confidentially. Retaliation against anyone who reports a violation in good faith is strictly prohibited.

## 5. Consequences of Violations

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. The severity of the action will depend on the nature of the violation.
