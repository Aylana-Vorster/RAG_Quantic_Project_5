
# Sandbox Onboarding Process

## 1. Welcome to Sandbox!

Our goal is to make your transition into your new role as smooth as possible. This document outlines the onboarding process for your first few weeks at Sandbox.

## 2. Before Your First Day

- **Offer Letter:** You will receive your official offer letter and employment contract via email. Please sign and return these documents before your start date.
- **Welcome Kit:** A welcome kit, including some Sandbox swag and your new laptop, will be shipped to you.

## 3. Your First Day

- **HR Orientation:** Your first day will begin with an orientation session with HR. We'll cover company policies, benefits, and get your payroll information sorted.
- **Meet Your Manager:** Your manager will introduce you to the team and give you an overview of your role and responsibilities.
- **IT Setup:** The IT team will help you get set up on our systems, including email, Slack, and our internal tools.

## 4. Your First Week: The 4 C's

Our onboarding program is structured around the "4 C's":

1.  **Compliance:** This is the basic level of onboarding. We make sure you have the tools and information you need to do your job and understand our policies.
2.  **Clarification:** We help you understand your role, how it fits into the team, and what is expected of you. You will work with your manager to set your 30-60-90 day goals.
3.  **Culture:** We introduce you to our company culture. Our CEO, Stacy Fakename, will host a welcome session for all new hires. You will also be assigned an "onboarding buddy" to help you navigate our culture and answer any questions you might have.
4.  **Connection:** We help you build relationships with your colleagues. We encourage you to have 1-on-1 meetings with your team members and key people in other departments.

## 5. Your First 30 Days

By the end of your first month, you should:

- Have a clear understanding of your role and responsibilities.
- Be familiar with our key tools and systems.
- Have met your team and other key stakeholders.
- Have a plan for what you will accomplish in your first 90 days.

## 6. Onboarding Checklist

A detailed onboarding checklist will be provided to you and your manager to track your progress through the onboarding process.

## 7. Feedback

We are always looking to improve our onboarding process. We will ask for your feedback at the end of your first 90 days.
