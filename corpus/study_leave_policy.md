
# Sandbox Study Leave Policy

## 1. Introduction

Sandbox is committed to the professional development of its employees. This policy outlines the provisions for study leave for employees who wish to pursue further education or training that is relevant to their role and career development at Sandbox.

## 2. Eligibility

- All permanent, full-time employees who have completed their probation period are eligible to apply for study leave.
- The course of study must be relevant to the employee's current role or future career path within the company.

## 3. Entitlement

- Eligible employees may be granted up to 10 days of paid study leave per academic year.
- This leave is intended for attending classes, writing exams, or other critical course-related activities.
- Study leave is granted at the discretion of management and is subject to business requirements.

## 4. Financial Assistance

- In addition to paid study leave, Sandbox may offer financial assistance for tuition and course fees.
- The level of financial assistance is determined on a case-by-case basis and requires approval from both the employee's manager and HR.
- Employees who receive financial assistance will be required to sign a training bond agreement. This agreement will require the employee to repay a pro-rated amount of the assistance if they leave the company within a specified period after completing the course.

## 5. Application Process

- Employees must submit a study leave application form to their manager at least one month before the start of the course.
- The application must include details of the course, the institution, the relevance to the employee's role, and a schedule of leave required.
- The manager will review the application and, if approved, forward it to HR for final approval.

## 6. Responsibilities of the Employee

- Employees on study leave are expected to maintain satisfactory academic progress.
- The employee must provide proof of registration and exam results to HR.
- Employees are expected to share the knowledge and skills gained with their teams.

## 7. Policy Questions

For any questions about the study leave policy, please contact Human Resources.
