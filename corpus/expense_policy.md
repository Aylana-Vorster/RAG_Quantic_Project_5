
# Sandbox Expense Policy

## 1. Introduction

This policy outlines the guidelines for business-related expenses and reimbursement. Sandbox will reimburse employees for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred while conducting company business.

## 2. General Principles

- **Reasonableness:** Expenses should be reasonable and not extravagant.
- **Business Purpose:** All expenses must have a clear business purpose.
- **Approval:** All expenses must be approved by your manager.

## 3. Reimbursable Expenses

Examples of reimbursable expenses include:

- **Travel:** Airfare, lodging, and ground transportation for business trips.
- **Meals:** Meals while traveling on business or for business meetings.
- **Client Entertainment:** Reasonable expenses for entertaining clients.
- **Home Office Stipend:** A monthly stipend may be provided to remote employees to cover a portion of home office expenses, such as internet and electricity.
- **Professional Development:** Costs for approved conferences, courses, and certifications.

## 4. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Examples of non-reimbursable expenses include:

- Personal travel
- Commuting costs to your primary office
- Traffic violations or parking tickets
- Childcare
- Airline club memberships

## 5. Expense Reporting

- **Submission:** All expense reports must be submitted through the company's expense management system.
- **Receipts:** Original, itemized receipts are required for all expenses.
- **Timeliness:** Expense reports should be submitted within 30 days of incurring the expense.

## 6. Corporate Credit Cards

Corporate credit cards may be issued to employees who travel frequently or have significant business expenses. The corporate card is to be used for business expenses only.

## 7. Policy Questions

If you have any questions about this policy or whether an expense is reimbursable, please contact your manager or the finance department before making the purchase.
