
# Sandbox Dress Code Policy

## 1. Our Philosophy

At Sandbox, we believe in fostering a professional and comfortable work environment. We trust our employees to use their best judgment when it comes to their attire. Our dress code is "smart casual."

## 2. What is Smart Casual?

Smart casual is a professional yet relaxed style. It means looking neat and put-together.

**Examples of appropriate smart casual attire:**

- **Tops:** Blouses, button-down shirts, sweaters, polo shirts, professional T-shirts.
- **Bottoms:** Chinos, trousers, smart jeans (no rips or tears), skirts.
- **Footwear:** Loafers, boots, clean sneakers, flats.

## 3. Client-Facing Roles

If you are meeting with clients or representing Sandbox at an external event, you are expected to dress in business professional attire. If you are unsure what is appropriate, err on the side of being more formal.

**Examples of business professional attire:**

- **Men:** Suit or blazer, dress shirt, trousers, and dress shoes. A tie is recommended.
- **Women:** Suit, dress, or a skirt/trousers with a blouse and blazer.

## 4. Unacceptable Attire

The following items are not considered appropriate for the workplace:

- Clothing with offensive or inappropriate graphics or language.
- Beachwear (e.g., flip-flops, shorts).
- Sportswear (e.g., tracksuits, gym clothes), unless you are participating in a company wellness activity.
- Clothing that is torn, dirty, or revealing.

## 5. Health and Safety

In certain areas, such as a server room or workshop, specific safety attire (like closed-toe shoes) may be required.

## 6. Flexibility

We understand that comfort is important. We aim for this policy to be a guideline, not a strict set of rules. However, management reserves the right to ask an employee to change if their attire is deemed inappropriate for the workplace.

If you have any questions, please speak with your manager or HR.
