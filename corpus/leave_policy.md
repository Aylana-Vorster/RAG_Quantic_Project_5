# Sandbox Leave Policy (South Africa)

## 1. Introduction

Sandbox is committed to providing employees with adequate leave to rest, recover from illness, and attend to personal matters. This policy is aligned with the Basic Conditions of Employment Act (BCEA) of South Africa. Our CEO, Stacy Fakename, believes that a healthy work-life balance is crucial for success.

## 2. Leave Year

Our leave year runs from January 1st to December 31st.

## 3. Annual Leave

- **Entitlement:** Full-time employees are entitled to 21 consecutive days of paid annual leave per year. This equates to 1.75 days for every month worked.
- **Granting Leave:** Leave must be taken at a time that is mutually agreeable to the employee and the company.
- **Accumulation:** Employees may accumulate a maximum of 30 days of annual leave. Any leave in excess of this must be taken within 6 months.
- **Payment in Lieu of Leave:** The company will not pay employees for annual leave that they do not take, except upon termination of employment.

## 4. Sick Leave

- **Entitlement:** In a 36-month cycle, employees are entitled to one day of paid sick leave for every 26 days worked. For a 5-day work week, this amounts to approximately 30 days of sick leave over three years.
- **Notification:** If you are unable to work due to illness, you must notify your manager as soon as reasonably possible.
- **Medical Certificate:** A medical certificate is required if you are absent from work for more than two consecutive days, or on more than two occasions during an eight-week period.

## 5. Family Responsibility Leave

- **Entitlement:** Employees who have worked for more than four months are entitled to 3 days of paid family responsibility leave per year.
- **Usage:** This leave can be taken when your child is born, when your child is sick, or in the event of the death of a spouse, life partner, parent, grandparent, child, or sibling.
- **Reasonable Proof:** The company may require reasonable proof of the event for which the leave is taken.

## 6. Study Leave

Sandbox supports the continuous development of its employees. A separate Study Leave policy is available with more details.

## 7. Public Holidays

Sandbox observes all official public holidays in South Africa.

## 8. Unpaid Leave

Unpaid leave may be granted at the discretion of management for situations not covered by this policy.

## 9. Policy Questions

For any questions, please speak to your manager or Human Resources.
