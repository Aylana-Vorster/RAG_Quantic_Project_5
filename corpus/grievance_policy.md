
# Sandbox Grievance Policy (South Africa)

## 1. Introduction

Sandbox is committed to creating a positive and fair work environment. This policy provides a formal process for employees to raise and resolve work-related grievances. This policy is in line with the principles of the Labour Relations Act of South Africa.

## 2. What is a Grievance?

A grievance is a concern, problem, or complaint that an employee has about their work, the workplace, or their relationship with a manager or colleague. Examples include:

- Issues with terms and conditions of employment.
- Health and safety concerns.
- Unfair treatment or discrimination.
- Issues with new working practices.

This procedure should not be used to appeal disciplinary decisions, which have a separate appeals process.

## 3. Informal Procedure

Before initiating a formal grievance, employees are encouraged to try and resolve the issue informally with their direct manager. An informal discussion can often resolve issues quickly.

## 4. Formal Grievance Procedure

**Step 1: Written Grievance**
- If an informal approach is not successful, the employee should put their grievance in writing and submit it to their manager or HR.
- The written grievance should clearly state the nature of the complaint and the desired outcome.

**Step 2: Grievance Hearing**
- A formal hearing will be scheduled within 5 working days of receiving the written grievance.
- The hearing will be chaired by a manager who is not involved in the grievance.
- The employee has the right to be accompanied by a fellow employee or a trade union representative.
- Both parties will have the opportunity to state their case.

**Step 3: Outcome**
- The chairperson will provide a written outcome within 5 working days of the hearing.
- The outcome will state the decision and the reasons for it.

**Step 4: Appeal**
- If the employee is not satisfied with the outcome, they may appeal the decision in writing to a more senior manager within 5 working days.
- An appeal hearing will be held, and the outcome of the appeal is final.

## 5. External Recourse

If the matter is still not resolved to the employee's satisfaction, they have the right to refer the dispute to the Commission for Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration (CCMA).

## 6. Confidentiality and Non-Retaliation

- All grievances will be handled with sensitivity and confidentiality.
- Sandbox prohibits any form of retaliation against an employee for raising a grievance in good faith.
