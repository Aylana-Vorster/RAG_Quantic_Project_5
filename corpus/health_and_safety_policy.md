
# Sandbox Health and Safety Policy

## 1. Introduction

Sandbox is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors. This policy is in compliance with the Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA) of South Africa. Our goal is to prevent workplace accidents and illnesses.

## 2. Management Commitment

Management, under the leadership of our CEO Stacy Fakename, is committed to:

- Providing the resources necessary to maintain a safe and healthy workplace.
- Ensuring compliance with all applicable health and safety legislation.
- Promoting a culture of safety throughout the organization.

## 3. Employee Responsibilities

All employees have a responsibility to:

- Take reasonable care of their own health and safety and that of others.
- Comply with all health and safety procedures and instructions.
- Report any unsafe conditions or practices to their manager or the Health and Safety Representative.
- Not interfere with or misuse anything provided for health and safety.

## 4. Health and Safety Representatives

- Health and Safety Representatives will be appointed for various work areas.
- These representatives will be responsible for conducting workplace inspections, investigating incidents, and representing employees on health and safety matters.
- A Health and Safety Committee will meet regularly to discuss and address health and safety issues.

## 5. Hazard Identification and Risk Assessment

- We will regularly conduct hazard identification and risk assessments to identify potential dangers in the workplace.
- Control measures will be implemented to eliminate or minimize these risks.

## 6. Emergency Procedures

- **Evacuation:** Evacuation routes are clearly marked, and all employees must familiarize themselves with them. Evacuation drills will be conducted regularly.
- **First Aid:** First aid kits are available in designated locations. A list of certified first aiders is available from HR.
- **Fire:** Fire extinguishers are located throughout the premises. Only trained personnel should attempt to use them.

## 7. Incident Reporting and Investigation

- All work-related accidents, injuries, and near misses must be reported to a manager immediately.
- All incidents will be investigated to determine the cause and to implement corrective actions to prevent a recurrence.

## 8. Remote Workstations

- Employees working from home are responsible for maintaining a safe and ergonomic workspace.
- A self-assessment checklist for home workstations will be provided.

## 9. Training

All employees will receive training on health and safety procedures, including emergency preparedness.
